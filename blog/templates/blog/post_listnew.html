What is Django Girls

  • non-profit organization and a community that empowers and helps women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources and support.
  • a volunteer run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology.
  • making technology more approachable by creating simpler tools and resources designed with empathy
  • During each event, 30-60 women build their first web application using HTML, CSS, Python and Django.
